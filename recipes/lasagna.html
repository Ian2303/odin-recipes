Lasagna Recipe

Description

Traditional Italian meal. Good family meal for warming everyone up

Ingredients

  • Pasta sheets
  • 500gm Mince
  • 1 Onion
  • Can of Tomatoes
  • 250ml White sauce
    • 260gm mixed shredded cheeses

Steps

  1. Fry the mince and onion whith some Olive oil until soft
    2. Add the can of tomatoes with herbs and spices to taste
  2. Line the bottom of a baking dish with pasta sheets
  3. Add a layer of the mince mix
